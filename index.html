Drig Drisya Viveka // Stage I
The Exterior Cosmos
Look closely at the space around you. Colors, textures, shapes, and the device in your hand. The forms are many, constantly moving and shifting, but your sight remains singular and steady. The world is an object observed by your physical senses.

Contemplation Gate:

Since a camera cannot photograph itself, and an object cannot see itself, can you be identical to the external world you are currently tracking? What separates your sight from the things you see?
